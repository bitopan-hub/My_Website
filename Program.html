Academic Programs

Master of Technology (M.Tech) in IT

Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

Eligibility: B.E./B.Tech. in CSE/IT or MCA/M.Sc. in CS/IT with a minimum of 60% marks. Valid GATE score required.

Overview: This program is designed to provide students with advanced knowledge in specialized areas of Information Technology. Core subjects include Advanced Algorithms, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, and Network Security. The final year is dedicated to a dissertation project.

Bachelor Of Technology(B.Tech) in CSE

Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

Eligibility: 10+2 Qualified in Science Stream, securing a minimum of 75% marks in Physics Chemestry And Mathematics.

Overview: The B.Tech. program provides a strong foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of CS. It covers subjects like Data Structures, Database Management Systems, Software Engineering, and Web Technologies. Students undertake a major project in their final semester.

Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in IT

Duration: 3-5 Years

Eligibility: M.Tech./M.Sc. in IT/CS or a related field with a strong academic record. Candidates must clear the university's research entrance test (GURET) or have a valid UGC/CSIR-NET/SLET qualification.

Overview: The Ph.D. program is for students interested in advanced research. Research areas include, but are not limited to, Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Bioinformatics, Network Security, and IoT. Scholars work under the guidance of experienced faculty members.
